About Me

I am a dedicated Assistant Professor and the Head of the Artificial Intelligence Department at Iqra University, Karachi. My passion lies in advancing AI to address practical challenges and creating impactful AI solutions. I have a broad spectrum of research interests that encompass healthcare, finance, creative technologies, and energy management. I am particularly involved in projects related to Explainable AI (XAI), multimodal data analysis, and real-time applications.

My research journey includes developing innovative solutions in various AI domains, particularly in areas like computer vision, deep learning, energy optimization, and machine learning algorithms. My research focuses on creating cutting edge AI solutions for real-world applications with a commitment to innovation, research excellence, and ethical practices.

I am also an enthusiastic educator, committed to providing an engaging and enriching learning environment for my students. I believe in the power of teaching and mentoring to inspire the next generation of innovators.
